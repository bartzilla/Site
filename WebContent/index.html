Welcome

My name is Cipriano Sanchez, I'm a Software Engineer currently located in Munich Germany. I've been working as a developer at hybris for the last few years; where we build enterprise software applications for multi-channel commerce. At work I mostly write Java code, along with its related set of tools & frameworks. Before joining hybris I lived and studied in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg where I earned a Master degree in Software Engineering at the Hochschule für technik Stuttgart.

I believe software engineers are empowered to truly change the world and we are doing it!. But to materialize brilliant ideas into software solutions it is necessary to have a solid technical foundation of how to build it. The good news is that this foundation of knowledge is in our hands and publicly available. I think that is a very exciting thing! and constantly motivates me to improve my knowledge and learn new things that eventually will provide me the toolset required to build amazing projects. That is partly the goal of this site. Besides of being a lab place where I'll get to test out new technologies, hopefully it will also be my contribution to share a bit of what I've learned. Mostly in the programming area, but probably I will be adding content of general interest too.

Besides technology, I also love traveling & music. I believe traveling and discovering new cultures is super fun and adds great value to people's life. That's why I try to visit new places as often as I possibly can, specially in summer when the weather is great and the sun sets after 10pm.

I really hope you can find interesting content in this site, you can always reach me at ciprianosanchez@gmail.com
