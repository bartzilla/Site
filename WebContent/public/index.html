Welcome

My name is Cipriano Sanchez. I'm a Software Engineer currently located in Munich Germany. I've been working as a developer at hybris for the last few years where we build enterprise software applications for multi-channel commerce. At work I mostly write Java code, along with its related set of tools & frameworks. Before joining hybris, I lived and studied in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg where I earned a Master's degree in Software Engineering at Hochschule für technik Stuttgart.

I believe software engineers are empowered to truly change the world and we are doing it!. But to materialize brilliant ideas into software solutions, it is necessary to have a solid technical foundation of how to build it. The good news is that this foundation of knowledge is in our hands and publicly available. I think that is a very exciting thing! It constantly motivates me to improve my knowledge and learn new things that will eventually provide me the toolset required to build amazing projects. That is partly the goal of this site. Besides being a lab place where I'll get to test out new technologies, hopefully it will also be my contribution to share a bit of what I've learned. I will probably be adding mostly programming related content, but I'll also include content of general interest, as well.

Besides technology, I also love traveling and music. I believe traveling and discovering new cultures is super fun and adds great value to people's life. That is why I try to visit new places as often as I possibly can, especially in summer when the weather is great and the sun sets after 10pm.

I really hope you can find interesting content in this site, you can always reach me at ciprianosanchez@gmail.com
